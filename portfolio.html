Rufus the Doofus:

This was my main project for my production design course. I wanted to create a toolbox that was specialized for a specific purpose. Here this one is specfically for wall repair. I decided to use my cartooning skills to my advantage in this to create something subliminally patronizing, since this product is meant to be used by people with no knowledge of tools.

Tote Bags:

For this project, my team and I created designs for tote bags that Student Government wanted to start handing out to students in order to eliminate plastic waste, along with some good media flyers to go with it. I thought it turned out pretty well!

Timebanking Airway Heights:

Here we were given the challenge of redesigning a logo and creating a flyer for a timebanking firm in Airway Heights. It was challenging because we had to learn what timebanking was and had to figure out how to work that into our designs. We also had to incorporate something having to do with Airway Heights into our design, which was also a challenge.

Shadle Park High School:

Here we had to create a new brand Identity for the Shadle Park High School Highlanders, who wanted a "gender neutral" mascot that didn't infringe copyright (their old mascot and logo got copyright stricken). We had to create something had that to do with the scottish highlands, because Highlanders, so...
